The Solar System

The Solar System is the Sun and all the objects that orbit around it

The Sun is orbited by planets, asteroids, comets and other things.
It is a star and it has very strong gravity.
The other objects are pulled into orbit around the Sun

The solar system is about 4.6 billion years old.
There are eight planets in the Solar System.
From closest to farthest from the Sun, they are:

Mercury Venus Earth Mars Jupiter Saturn Uranus Neptune

The first four planets are called terrestrial planets andhey are mostly made of rock and metal, and they are mostly solid

The last four planets are called gas giants, this is because they are much larger than other planets and are mostly made of gas

The Solar System also contains other things like asteroid belts, mostly between Mars and Jupiter. There are also comets, centaurs, and there is interplanetary dust

Many other systems like the Solar System have been found. Each of the billions of stars in the Milky Way galaxy might have a planetary system
